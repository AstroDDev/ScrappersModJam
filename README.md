**Scrappers** is a minigame plugin where you have to survive and shoot waves of enemies, collect their scraps, and fulfill the quota before the end of the wave.

This game can be played solo or with friends !

Every time you win or lose a game, the difficulty of the next instance will increase or decrease. Try to hit your high score! 

Note: The uncompressed assets can be viewed at : https://github.com/AstroDDev/ScrappersModJamWorld

---

## Gameplay

- **Survive and shoot:** Fight off waves of enemies.
- **Collect scraps:** Gather dropped scraps from defeated enemies to reach the wave quota.
- **Dynamic difficulty:** The game difficulty increase each round and each games !

You can view and change the difficulty of the minigame before starting:

```
/scrappers getdifficulty
/scrappers setdifficulty 2.0
```
